INTERACTIVE CONTROLS (Mode 1):
====================

MAIN MENU (each turn):
[1] Move (WASD)
[2] Attack (current target)
[3] Change Weapon
[4] View Status  
[5] Pass Turn
[6] Help

MOVEMENT CONTROLS (WASD):
W - Move UP (North, +Y)
A - Move LEFT (West, -X)
S - Move DOWN (South, -Y)
D - Move RIGHT (East, +X)

Then enter distance (e.g., "2.5")

WEAPON SELECTION:
[1] Bow - Range:4, Power:1 (safe, long distance)
[2] Spear - Range:2, Power:2 (balanced)
[3] Sword - Range:1, Power:4 (high risk, high reward)

STRATEGIC TIPS:
===============
1. KEEP DISTANCE with Bow (enemies can't reach you)
2. USE SWORD when enemies get close (maximum damage)
